About DMD Electrical

DMD Electrical is a family owned and operated business with over 10 years of industrial and commercial expertise. We service areas in and around Gauteng and its neighbouring provinces. As an owned business we value the importance of our reputation and trust with our customers.

We treat each of our clients with the utmost respect, patience, professionalism. Each task we complete expresses our commitment to high quality workmanship and attention to detail.

Our Expertise

Commercial troubleshooting and electrical repair is available on a 24 hour emergency basis, and with an in-house parts and repair inventory.

DMD Electrical is fully equipped to handle all forms of commercial electrical projects for both small and large businesses.
